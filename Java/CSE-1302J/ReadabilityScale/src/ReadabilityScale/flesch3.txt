LOS ANGELES  
The home of Paris and Nicky Hilton has been burglarized.  Jewelry, watches and a laptop were stolen.  
Nicky Hilton returned home early Thursday morning to discover the break-in.  It appears to have 
occurred sometime after 9 o'clock on Wednesday night.  Police did not place a dollar value on the 
items stolen. The spokeswoman described it as an high amount.  The contents of the laptop were 
not released.  Nicky Hilton, 19, told officers that no suspects were seen.  The case remains under 
investigation. She could not confirm a report that the home had been ransacked.  Paris Hilton stars 
with Nicole Richie in the Fox television show The Simple Life.  The 23-year-old heiress is also 
recording an album.  She recently finished work on her feature film debut.  It is a remake 
of the horror film House of Wax.
